3:T72c,## Student Travel Support

To support the participation of early-career researchers, Beyond IID 14 offers travel support for students from overseas universities. This support includes local accommodation and a waiver of the conference registration fee.


**Eligibility:** Applications are open to students from overseas universities. Priority will be given to:

- authors or co-authors of accepted papers or posters at Beyond IID 14 who have limited financial means to attend the conference; and
- applicants who can clearly demonstrate the academic benefits of attending Beyond IID 14.

**Application Process:** To apply for student travel support, please send an email to [beyondiid2026@gmail.com](mailto:beyondiid2026@gmail.com) using the following subject line:

- **Application for Student Travel Support - Full Name - Affiliation**

Your application email should include:

- your full name, affiliation, academic level, email address, and your supervisor's email address;
- a brief statement describing your financial need;
- your CV, attached as a PDF, including your educational background and scientific outputs; and
- a signed supporting statement from your supervisor, attached as a PDF, confirming your eligibility, recommending your attendance at Beyond IID 14, and indicating the supervisor's commitment to providing additional support for your participation, such as round-trip airfare.

**Deadline and Notification:** The application deadline is **May 11, 2026, AoE**. Late or incomplete applications will not be considered. Successful applicants will be notified by **May 13, 2026**.
