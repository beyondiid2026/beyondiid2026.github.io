Method 2: WeChat Pay

WeChat (Weixin) Pay is one of the most widely used payment methods in China and can be used in almost all shops, restaurants, and other everyday situations. It is often more convenient than carrying cash or using a physical bank card. We encourage all participants to set up WeChat Pay before traveling to China, as it will make life much easier during your stay.

There are many online guides that explain how to download WeChat, register an account, and link it to your bank card. You should be able to find one that matches your situation easily.

Once your WeChat account is set up and connected to your bank card, you can follow the step-by-step instructions below to pay the registration fee.
